## Kisi-kisi

```
echo hostname uname date cal who
cd pwd ls touch
cp mv rm mkdir rmdir
chmod ln
locate find wc
cat head tail sort uniq cut tr grep sed
ps nice renice pidof kill
& jobs fg bg
```
